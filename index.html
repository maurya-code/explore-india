INDIA

India the Great Nation


India is one of the oldest civilizations in the world with a kaleidoscopic variety and rich cultural heritage.The history of india is shrouded in antiquity. India is one of the cradles of civilization with a history dating back more than 9000 years. As the 7th largest country in the world, India stands apart from the rest of Asia, marked off as it is by mountains and the sea, which give the country a distinct geographical entity. Bounded by the Great Himalayas in the north, it stretches southwards and at the Tropic of Cancer, tapers off into the Indian Ocean between the Bay of Bengal on the east and the Arabian Sea on the west. As you travel, India offers a range of vast tourism choices, diverse in land and nature, people, tribes, cuisine, faiths, dance forms, music, arts, crafts, adventure, sport, spirituality, history; even these vary as you journey from one state to another.


Its landscape ranging from snow-capped mountain ranges to deserts, plains, rainforests , hills, plateaus and much more which gives you an opportunity to explore diverse destinations of North, South, East and West India, comprising nature destinations of hill stations, beaches, desert, backwaters, mountain, trekking, wildlife and adventure.


Not just this, India is heaven for the foodie traveller. You’ll be pleasantly surprised by the myriad range of cuisines you can try your hands at. Every state has a bunch of local specialties to offer. Dal baati churma of Rajasthan, butter chicken of Punjab,kachauri, and samosa of Uttar Pradesh, vada pav of Maharashtra and several varieties of dosa of Kerala will leave you craving for more. Other than these, there is never a dearth of multi-cuisine restaurants to tantalize your taste buds.


India is synonymous with festivals, celebrations and lots of colour, so any time of the year is a good time to visit. Try going to some of the popular attractions like the Taj Mahal in Agra, Golden Temple in Amritsar, Gateway of India in Mumbai. You are sure to get mesmerized by the unique traditions and warmth of the locals anywhere you go in the country.

DISCOVER INDIA

Land of festivals

The diversity of Indian culture is not hidden from the rest of the world. The massive celebration of the festivals of India, the traditions, and rituals all calls in for the tourists from all around the globe.One can enjoy the festival celebration in India every month. Each and every festival is celebrated uniquely in different ways according to the rituals, beliefs and its significant history behind. Each festival has its own history, legend and significance of celebration

Variety Of Dance Forms

India is a land of varied cultures and traditions. Even the dance forms of India has so much rich heritage. Dance is India is much more than mere body movements, from the very ancient times the classical dance forms is considered as a discipline and a way to devote yourself to the God through art. India has many dances in India coming from every state in the country.

Variety of Delicious Food

India's regional and cultural diversity reflects beautifully in its food and is possibly the main reason why Indian food outranks that of other countries. Each Indian state has its own unique pandora of flavours and ingredients. Even the spices they use are their own concoction. Prepare yourself to dive into a world of spice-packed, flavour and fragrance rich Indian food.

Diverse Terrain

The geography of India is greatly diverse, with landscape ranging from snow-capped mountain ranges to deserts, plains, hills and plateaus. India comprises most of the Indian subcontinent situated on the Indian Plate, the northerly portion of the Indo-Australian Plate. Having a coastline of over 7,000 km (4,300 miles), most of India lies on a peninsula in southern Asia that protrudes into the Indian Ocean. India is bounded in the southwest by the Arabian Sea and in the southeast by the Bay of Bengal.

ACTIVITIES TO DO

SAFARI

With more than 99 national parks, 40 tiger reserves and 450 wildlife sanctuaries, India has cosseted enthralling species of fauna and flora. Some important destinations to visit are : Ranthambore National Park, Jim Corbett National Park,Kanha National Park, Kaziranga National Park, Sunderban National Park ...

SKIING

The rugged Indian Himalaya has become an arena for many adventure activities like trekking, motor biking, cycling and skiing. Manali (Himachal Pradesh),Auli (Uttarakhand),Gulmarg and Kufri are some poular skiing destinations.

TREKKING

Trekking in India gives you innumerable opportunities to stay close to nature, and explore the charm of the geographical diversities of the country. Popular Treks in India:Valley of Flowers,Roopkund,Hampta Pass,Har Ki Doon and Kashmir Great Lakes

PARA-GLIDING

India is a land of infinite possibilities.India is home to some of the most beautiful gliding destinations and sites. some of the breathtaking beautiful paragliding sites are: Bir Billing, Manali, Mussoorie, Bangalore, Goa ...

RAFTING

With a plethora of rivers within reach, India is becoming a hotspot for river rafting. Best places for river rafting in India:River Ganga at Rishikesh,Teesta River at Sikkim and Darjeeling and Indus River at Ladakh
